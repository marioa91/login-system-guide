Complete Explanation

Login systems are designed to provide both access and security. When users enter their details, the system verifies them through backend servers. If matched, access is granted immediately.

Modern systems also use session management and tokens to maintain login state without repeated verification. This improves user experience while maintaining safety.

Understanding these basics helps users avoid errors and use the platform efficiently.
